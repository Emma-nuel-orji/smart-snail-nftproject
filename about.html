FAQ

* How valuable can Smart Snail NFT get?

If we’ve done a good job in communicating Smart Snail NFT, then by now you should understand we are only interested in playing longterm games. We are not interested in selling you jpegs and leaving you to your fate but in giving these NFTs real life values. We are not interested in selling out immediately, but in organic community growth which will attract like minds that way we ensure long term sustainability of this project. There are many factors that will make Smart Snail very valuable, First, it provides access to multiple services and benefits, including gym memberships, rent payment, and tourism experiences, depending on the staking duration. This creates a level of convenience and flexibility for the consumer, which can make the NFT more attractive and valuable. Secondly, the staking mechanism provides a way for users to earn rewards for holding onto the NFT for a certain period. This can create an incentive for users to acquire and hold onto the NFT, which can increase its value over time. Thirdly, the unique design and limited supply of the NFT can also contribute to its value. Overall, the combination of utility, staking rewards, and unique design will make this NFT valuable and attractive to a wide range of users and collectors.


* Where would the funds used to pay gyms, tour experiences property rents, hotel bills or content providers come from?

We would have reserve funds from where we can service our platform. For most part of it the SS1 DApp is self-sufficient in the sense that ticket fees paid in crypto are splitted with some percentages going to the platform while the rest goes to the reserve fund. Then for the access grant by staking NFT, the staking rewards will be sent to the reserve funds. The funds for paying gym, rent, and hotels would come from the staked Smart Snail SS2 NFTs. Users would stake their NFTs for a specific duration, and in return, they would receive staking rewards in the form of cryptocurrency. These rewards would be used to pay for gym memberships, rent, or hotel stays, depending on the duration of the staking period chosen by the user. For example, if a user stakes their Smart Snail NFT for 6 months, they would receive a staking reward of let’s say $100. This reward would be a split with a percentage going to the platform and the rest would then be used to pay for rent, as long as the landlord is part of the platform, same goe for the gym, short let or hotel stay. We would also source revenue from transaction fees and secondary sale fees which go to the receive fund. We will make room for advertisements and partnerships where percentages of these deals go to the reserve fund. We are also going to buy more plots in TCG and other metaverses which serve as branches thereby having more reach and revenue. We would engage in TCG farming by buying farming plots. Farming in TCG is a mechanism where you plant seeds otherwise crypto and tend to your farming plot which grow and multiply, this is one of the revenue streams we would be employing.


* How do we plan getting film makers, creators, gym, property owners, tour guides and hoteliers onboard especially in countries without our presence?

To onboard gym, property owners, and hoteliers our first approach is targeted advertisement, we would use a program where we source for individuals who are enthusiast or involved in the relevant fields who we call Reps or Ambassadors. Provide the ambassadors with information about the Smart Snail NFT and the DApp, benefits, and how it can be used by gym, Tourism, property owners, and hoteliers. Create a referral program that rewards ambassadors for successfully onboarding partners to the platform. Offer incentives to gym, property owners, tour and hoteliers for joining the platform, such as reduced fees or increased visibility to potential customers. Provide training and support to gym, property owners, and hoteliers on how to use the platform effectively. Continuously engage with gym houses, tour guides, property owners, and hoteliers to gather feedback and improve the platform. How do we plan to address potential challenges such as cryptocurrency price volatility and concerns of staking rewards not being enough to cover the service fees like rent, gym membership, hotel bill or tour fees etc By implementing strategies such as setting a floor on staking rewards can help ensure that stakers receive a minimum level of rewards, even if market conditions are unfavorable. Establishing a reserve fund can also help mitigate risks by providing a cushion of funds to cover unexpected expenses or losses. We would regularly monitor and adjust the program: It's important to regularly monitor the costs and benefits of the program, and make adjustments as needed to ensure that it remains financially sustainable over the long term.
